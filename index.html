Welcome to JEKEJEKE MADADRIVE!
JEKEJEKE MADADRIVE is an independent entity.Its activity is based on Vehicle Rental and Professional Tour Provider. The word "JEKEJEKE" is a local dialect used by the Betsileo South (Ambalavao), meaning "Moramora" in official Malagasy and "serenely or calmly" in French. MADA = Madagascar "DRIVE" is an English word that means to drive (a car).
Thus, we can translate JEKEJEKE MADADRIVE as "driving a car with serenity in Madagascar."
The inspiration comes from creating a sole proprietorship for oneself without depending on anyone. His existence contributes to providing jobs for others.
