ADVICE

CHANGE

Change: The ARIARY (MGA or Ar) is the country's currency, but the old Malagasy Franc (Fmg) is still mentioned in some businesses.

Bank : You can make withdrawals with VISA and MasterCard at bank branches in major cities.

The exchange rate can vary from day to day, on average: 1 Euro = 4,900 Ar / 1 Dollar = 4,300 Ar / 1 British Pound = 5,200 Ar

The tickets in Ariary : 20 000 Ar / 10 000 Ar / 5 000 Ar / 2 000 Ar / 1 000 Ar / 500 Ar / 200 Ar / 100 Ar

ADVICE

SECURITY

As with any developing country, petty crime exists, especially in large cities. We recommend being vigilant during your hikes and travels, it is advisable to:

  • Do not flaunt signs of wealth around your wrists and in your pockets.
  • If you are staying in a tourist area, we advise you to keep your valuables in hotel safes. Be extremely vigilant.

ADVICE

HEALTH & HYGIENE

- Consult the primary care physician before your appointment to prescribe appropriate medication.
- Malaria and yellow fever are still widespread in Madagascar, so antimalarial tablets and the use of mosquito nets in bedrooms are recommended.
- Think about sunscreen, mosquito repellent creams, hand sanitizers...

Clothing and miscellaneous:
- Warm clothing and hiking gear such as good walking shoes, cap, flashlight, trekking poles, swimsuit, raincoat, umbrella..
- Pack a few clothes in your carry-on luggage in case of any potential issues (lost or redirected luggage...)
- Plan for contact lenses in case of need

ADVICE

CLIMATE

The climate is generally of the humid tropical type, which is divided into 2 parts:

Southern Winter (June-August): generally cool in the highlands, temperate on the coasts, and very humid in the extreme North, the East coast of the island, and St. Marie.

Southern Summer (mid-December-April): the rains are abundant, risk of cyclones from December to March.

There is an off-season from May to September. The climate is generally temperate and dry.

ADVICE

TABOO OR FADY

The "Fady" constitute the set of prohibitions or taboos of Malagasy Culture.
Recommendations to be followed in sacred places and sites, he will show his interest in Malagasy culture.

The "Fady" is not only about an attitude, but also about prohibitions that can be related to clothing, a day of access, or dietary customs...

Your driver or guide will provide instructions and related anecdotes.

ADVICE

DIVERS & CUSTOMS

Entrance to National Park or Reserve: from 45,000 Ariary to 65,000 Ariary per day for foreigners per person,

Guiding depends on the duration, circuit, number of people, see on-site.

Restoration :Breakfast ranges from 6,000 Ar to 20,000 Ar, and the main dish ranges from 6,000 Ar to 50,000 Ar depending on the restaurant.

Hotel : The price of a room varies between 80,000 Ar to 300,000 Ar per night.

Tip : not mandatory, it's customary in Madagascar, depends on your will and satisfaction

Customs :All exports of raw precious stones, protected endemic plants, animals, and cultural objects are prohibited. They will be seized by Customs. However, you have the right to bring back artisanal products.
