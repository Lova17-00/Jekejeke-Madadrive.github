HIKING ESSENTIALS

CIRCUIT RN7

It's a National Road in Madagascar that connects Antananarivo to Tuléar. It takes us from the central plateaus to the beaches of the Southwest coast.
We offer you a 10-day Classic Tour.

Itinerary: Day 1: Tanà _Atsirabe / Day 2: Antsirabe _Ranomafana / Day 3: Ranomafana / Day 4: Ranomafana _Tsaranoro Valley / Day 5: Tsaranoro Valley / Day 6: Tsaranoro Valley _Isalo / D7: Isalo / D8: Isalo _Ifaty / D9: Ifaty / D10: Ifaty _Tuléar and Return to Tanà by Plane or Car

Note: The itinerary is adjustable according to your request and availability.

HIKING ESSENTIALS

WESTERN CIRCUIT

Our Western Circuit is based on the discovery of the Avenue of the Baobabs and the Tsingy de Bemaraha, which are part of the UNESCO World Heritage, with or without the descent of the Tsiribihina River by canoe according to your availability.
On this note, we offer you 10 days with the descent of the Tsiribihina River or 8 days without the descent of the Tsiribihina River.


Itinerary with descent: Day 1: Tanà _Atsirabe / Day 2: Antsirabe _Miandrivazo / Day 3 / Day 4: Descent on the Tsiribihina River/ D5: End of the descent _Bekopaka /D6: Tsingy de Bemaraha /D7: Bekopaka _Kirindy /D8: Kirindy _Morondava / D9: Morondava _Antsirabe /D10: Antsirabe _Tanà

HIKING ESSENTIALS

COMBINED CIRCUIT

This is a long circuit visiting the Andasibe National Park in the East, passing through the Avenue of the Baobabs and the Tsingy de Bemaraha without or with the descent of the Tsiribihina River, and the RN7 (Ranomafana, Anjà, Tsaranoro Valley, Isalo) up to Ifaty known for its beautiful beaches.

On this note, we offer you 20 days with the descent of the Tsiribihina River or 18 days without the descent of the Tsiribihina River.

HIKING ESSENTIALS

LOOP CIRCUIT

It's a long and combined circuit making a loop. It allows you to discover the Andasibe National Park in the East, the Avenue of the Baobabs, and the Tsingy of Bemaraha without or with the descent of the Tsiribihina River in the West, Belo sur Mer, Ifaty in the Southwest (you can extend the coastal route by passing through Andavadoaka, Salary depending on your availability), and then we will finish along the RN7 towards the Capital, crossing the landscapes of the highlands without forgetting the visits: Isalo, Tsaranoro Valley, Anjà, Ranomafana.
