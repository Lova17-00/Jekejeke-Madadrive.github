Picking the right Hiking Gear!

The nice thing about beginning hiking is that you don't really need any special gear, you can probably get away with things you already have.

Let's start with clothing. A typical mistake hiking beginners make is wearing jeans and regular clothes, which will get heavy and chafe as they get sweaty or wet.
